Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 17th March 2026

1. Introduction

At Trigzi, your privacy is our foundational principle. We build tools that help you understand your health, not tools that harvest your data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you use the Trigzi application.

2. Data Collection and Storage

Local-First Architecture: Trigzi is designed to operate locally. All health data, trigger logs, symptoms, and habits you enter are stored securely and exclusively on your device. We do not have access to your personal data, nor do we store it on any proprietary external servers.

3. Device Permissions

To provide core functionality, Trigzi requests the following device permissions:

  • Camera & Photos: Required for logging meals and utilizing Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to read ingredient labels. All image processing is performed locally on your device.

  • Storage: Required to save your logs, app preferences, and temporary cache data directly to your hardware.

4. Third-Party Services and Analytics

We do not integrate third-party advertising networks or tracking SDKs. We only utilize anonymized, aggregate crash reporting provided directly by your operating system (if you have explicitly opted-in via your device settings) to help us identify and fix technical issues.

5. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Because Trigzi does not collect or transmit your personal data to our servers, you inherently possess absolute control over your information. You can permanently delete all your data at any time using the data deletion tools within the app settings, or simply by uninstalling the application.

6. Children's Privacy

Trigzi does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Given our local-first design, no user data of any age is transmitted back to us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@trigzi.com.
